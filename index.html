About us

The Fur Retreat is a tight-knit, brand new group of furs (and non-furs) to hang out regardless of their species!

Our server has features such as:

  • Gender, Sexuality and Species Roles
  • Artist Channels
  • Roleplay Channels
  • Music Channels
  • Voice Chats
  • XP/Currency System
  • Giveaways!

Rules

  • Be civil with everyone.
  • Act maturely, and reasonably.
  • Only post content where it belongs.
  • Do not spam.
  • Do not advertise.
  • Nickname & profile picture (PFP) changes must be appropriate.
  • Act appropriately in the voice channels.
  • No NSFW content.
  • You must be 13 years old or above in order to use this server.
  • English only.
